## üöÄ Step-by-Step Submission Process

### Step 1: Access Google Search Console
1. Go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console/)
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Select your property: `https://toiral.com`

### Step 2: Navigate to Sitemaps Section
1. In the left sidebar, click on **"Sitemaps"**
2. You'll see the sitemaps overview page

### Step 3: Submit Your Sitemap
1. In the "Add a new sitemap" section
2. Enter: `sitemap.xml` (this will reference your updated sitemap)
3. Click **"SUBMIT"**

### Step 4: Verify Submission
1. Wait a few minutes for processing
2. Check the status - it should show "Success"
3. Monitor the "Discovered URLs" count
